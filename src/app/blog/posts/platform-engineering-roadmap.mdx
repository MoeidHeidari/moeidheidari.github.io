---
title: 'Platform Engineering Roadmap for Growing Teams'
publishedAt: '2023-07-02'
summary: 'How to introduce platform engineering in stages, from developer pain mapping to reusable internal products.'
---

Platform engineering is most effective when it solves concrete developer pain first. Start small, measure impact, then expand to broader internal capabilities.

## Stage 1: Discover friction

Interview teams and map repeated pain points:

- Slow environment setup
- Inconsistent deployment flows
- Weak observability defaults

## Stage 2: Build internal products

Treat platform capabilities as products:

- Clear ownership
- Documented APIs
- Roadmaps with adoption metrics

## Stage 3: Standardize guardrails

Automate security and reliability checks in CI/CD so teams get safe defaults without manual overhead.

## Result

A successful platform increases delivery speed and reduces cognitive load across teams.
